## Start here: download the software

- **Tropy** — manage, describe, and annotate research photographs.
  [https://tropy.org](https://tropy.org)

- **Obsidian** — a free, local-first note-taking app built on plain text files.
  [https://obsidian.md](https://obsidian.md)

## Download a starter Obsidian database and templates
